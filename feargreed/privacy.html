Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026
App: Fear & Greed Index
Developer: kicucya

This Privacy Policy applies to Fear & Greed Index on iOS and macOS.

Data We Collect

Fear & Greed Index does not require account registration and does not collect personal data directly from users.

The app:

  • Fetches market sentiment data from public APIs to display stock and crypto Fear & Greed information
  • Stores app settings, local cache, and widget-related state on your device using local storage such as UserDefaults and App Group storage
  • May schedule local notifications if notification reminders are enabled

Advertising

The app may display ads through Google AdMob where available. Google and its partners may collect data such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Coarse location derived from IP address
  • Crash and performance data
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Data

Data is used only to:

  • Provide the app's core functionality
  • Save local preferences and cached content
  • Deliver ads and measure ad performance
  • Improve app stability and reliability

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

  • App settings and cache remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the data through system behavior
  • Advertising-related data is handled according to Google's own policies

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in system settings at any time
  • You can manage ad and privacy preferences through your device settings and any consent flow provided by the app or Google

Children's Privacy

Fear & Greed Index is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised date.
