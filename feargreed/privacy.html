Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026
App: Fear & Greed Index Widget
Developer: kicucya

This Privacy Policy applies to Fear & Greed Index Widget on iOS and macOS.

Data Processing

Fear & Greed Index Widget does not upload your data to the developer's servers.

All data is processed locally on your device:

  • Fetching market sentiment data from public APIs
  • Displaying Fear & Greed Index via widgets and the app
  • Storing settings and cache (UserDefaults, App Group)
  • Scheduling local notifications (if enabled)

Third-Party Advertising

This app may display ads through Google AdMob where available. Google may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Coarse location derived from IP
  • Ad interaction data

See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Retention

  • App settings and cache remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the data
  • Advertising-related data is handled according to Google's own policies

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in system settings at any time
  • You can manage ad and privacy preferences through your device settings and any consent flow provided by the app or Google

Children's Privacy

Fear & Greed Index Widget is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised date.
