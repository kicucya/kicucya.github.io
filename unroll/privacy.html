Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026
App: unroll
Developer: kicucya

This Privacy Policy applies to unroll on iOS.

Data Processing

unroll does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer's servers.

All processing is done locally on your device:

  • Importing and stitching screenshots and videos
  • OCR text recognition
  • App settings storage (UserDefaults)
  • Exporting images to Photos (requires permission)

Third-Party Advertising

This app displays ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Coarse location derived from IP
  • Ad interaction data

See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Retention

  • Imported media and generated results are handled on your device
  • Items saved to the Photos library remain there until you delete them
  • Advertising-related data is handled according to Google's own policies

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke Photos permission in system settings
  • You can manage ad and privacy preferences through your device settings and any consent flow provided by the app or Google

Children's Privacy

unroll is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: kicu.cya+dev@gmail.com
