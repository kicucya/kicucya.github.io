Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026
App: unroll
Developer: kicucya

This Privacy Policy applies to unroll on iOS.

Data We Collect

unroll does not require account registration and does not upload user screenshots or videos to the developer's servers.

The app:

  • Processes imported screenshots and videos locally on your device
  • Performs OCR locally on your device
  • Saves app settings and local state using on-device storage such as UserDefaults
  • Saves exported images to your photo library only when you choose to do so and grant permission

Advertising

The app may display ads through Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect data such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Coarse location derived from IP address
  • Crash and performance data
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Data

Data is used only to:

  • Provide screenshot stitching, editing, export, and OCR features
  • Save local preferences and app state
  • Deliver ads and measure ad performance
  • Improve app stability and reliability

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

  • Imported media and generated results are handled on your device
  • Items saved to the Photos library remain there until you delete them
  • Advertising-related data is handled according to Google's own policies

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke Photos permission in system settings
  • You can manage ad and privacy preferences through your device settings and any consent flow provided by the app or Google

Children's Privacy

unroll is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised date.

Contact
